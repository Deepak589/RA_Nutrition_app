# Roadmap

The RA Nutrition & Lifestyle App is being built in three phases. Each phase builds on the last, moving from a solid logging foundation toward richer insight and, finally, connection with a user's wider care context.

> This roadmap describes product direction at a feature level. Timelines are indicative and subject to change.

---

## V1 — Foundation

Establish the core experience: make logging effortless and give users immediate, simple value.

- **Meal logging**
  - Record meals and individual foods
  - Searchable food library
  - Reusable meal templates / library entries
- **Symptom tracking**
  - Daily logging of RA-related symptoms (pain, stiffness, fatigue, flares)
  - Personal symptom history
- **Rule-based recommendations**
  - Transparent, explainable lifestyle suggestions
  - Non-clinical and general in nature
- **Weekly analytics**
  - Visual summaries of meals and symptoms
  - At-a-glance weekly trends
- **Accounts & authentication**
  - Secure sign-up and login
  - Personal, private data per user

---

## V2 — Insight

Move beyond logging into understanding. Help users connect what they eat with how they feel.

- **Richer analytics**
  - Longer time ranges (monthly, custom)
  - Deeper breakdowns of meal and symptom data
- **Trend correlations**
  - Surface relationships between dietary patterns and symptom trends
  - Highlight notable changes over time
- **Personalized goals**
  - User-defined habit and logging goals
  - Progress tracking against goals
- **Improved recommendations**
  - More contextual, history-aware suggestions
  - Clearer explanations of why a suggestion appears
- **Quality-of-life improvements**
  - Faster logging flows
  - Better food search and favorites

---

## V3 — Connection

Extend the app outward — toward sharing, reporting, and integration with the rest of a user's routine.

- **Care-team sharing**
  - Optionally share summaries with a healthcare provider or caregiver
  - User-controlled, privacy-first sharing
- **Exportable reports**
  - Downloadable summaries (e.g. PDF) of meals, symptoms, and trends
  - Useful for appointments and personal records
- **Reminders & habit nudges**
  - Gentle prompts to log meals and symptoms
  - Habit-building notifications
- **Integrations**
  - Connections with complementary health and wellness tools
  - Broader data import/export options

---

> ⚠️ All features remain **non-clinical lifestyle support**. The app does not provide medical advice — see the Medical Disclaimer in the [README](../README.md).
